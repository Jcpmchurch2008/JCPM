About Us

Our Mission:

At JCPM Church , our mission is to glorify God by making disciples who know Christ, grow in Christ, and go for Christ. We are committed to living out the Great Commission and sharing the love of Jesus with our community and the world.

Our History:

Founded in 2008 , JCPM CHURCH has been a beacon of hope and faith. Our journey began with a small group of believers and has grown into a vibrant congregation dedicated to worship, community service, and spiritual growth.

Our Beliefs:

The Bible: We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God.

God: We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation: We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received by faith in Jesus Christ.

The Church: We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God and serve others.
